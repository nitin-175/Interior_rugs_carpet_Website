A Legacy of Craftsmanship

For over four decades, Interior Rugs has been at the forefront of luxury carpet craftsmanship. Founded in Mumbai in 1985, we began our journey with a simple mission: to preserve the ancient art of hand-weaving while creating contemporary masterpieces that define modern luxury.

Our master artisans, many of whom represent generations of weaving families, work with the finest materials sourced from around the world. Each rug tells a story of dedication, skill, and passion that has been passed down through centuries.
